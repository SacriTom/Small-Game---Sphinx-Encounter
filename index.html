The Five Riddles
of the Celestial Sphinx

Across the void of stars you have come, seeker, to stand before the Gate of the Ancient Cosmos.

An eternal sphinx guards the passage to treasures beyond reckoning.
Answer five sacred riddles — each darker than the last — and you shall pass.
Doubt yourself but once, and her eyes shall open…
